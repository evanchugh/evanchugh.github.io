Selected Research Contributions

Applications of Convolutional Neural Networks in Echocardiogram Analysis
Evan Chugh
Independently established a relationship between Canisius College and a local medical practice. Gained experience with IRB and HIPAA guidelines. Created a utility to scrub studies of PHI. Worked with doctors to label high risk indicators of heart disease, then constructed and trained a convolutional neural network to be used in future patient screenings.
Electroskip: Using Machine Learning to Improve Patient Response in Physical Therapy
Evan Chugh, Debra Burhans, Jon Mrowczynski
Investigated the use of recurrent neural networks in creating real-time responses to human motion. Used gait and pressure data for detection of patients with early onset Parkinson’s Disease. Implemented a data collection system into the existing application pipeline. A study based on this work has shown moderate success in correcting the gait of patients with Parkinson’s Disease.
